VIP Terminal & VIP Lounge

The VIP Terminal & VIP Lounge service is offered by VIP Travel Agencies for their most discerning clients who seek a luxury travel experience without stress. This service aims to provide a range of exclusive benefits and conveniences for travelers, ensuring a personalized and high-class travel experience. Here's how the service offered by a VIP Travel Agency could be structured:

  • Exclusive Access to the VIP Terminal: Customers of the VIP Travel Agency can enjoy exclusive access to a dedicated VIP terminal inside the airport. This terminal offers a quiet and private environment away from the confusion and long queues of the airport's main terminals.
  • Exclusive Access to the VIP Lounge: In addition to the VIP Terminal, customers of the VIP Travel Agency can access exclusive VIP lounges inside the airport. These lounges offer a wide range of comforts, including gourmet buffets, well-stocked bars, work and relaxation spaces, as well as services such as spas and massages.
  • Personal Assistance: VIP Travel Agency can provide dedicated personal assistance to its clients throughout the travel process. This may include a personal travel agent available to assist with trip planning, reservations, special requests, and any issues that may arise during the journey.

VIP Terminal & VIP Lounge include :

Arrival

  • Our agent meets the passengers at the end of the airbridge or at the entrance to the terminal with a personalized name board
  • Escorting through passport control and customs using Fast Track Lanes wherever available
  • Escorting to baggage claim hall, help with the luggage if porter service is pre-booked
  • Escorting to onward mode of transportation or arrival hall, up to the clients needs

Departure

  • Our agent and passenger's driver/lead passenger get in contact to coordinate the drop-off time and place prior to their arrival
  • Meeting passengers curbside by the agent and baggage porter if pre-booked
  • Escorting through all check-in and security formalities using Fast Track Lanes wherever available
  • Escorting to the Departure Lounge if arranged and/or to the boarding gate

Transit

  • Our agent meets the passengers at the end of the airbridge or at the entrance to the terminal with a personalized name board
  • Escorting to baggage claim if needed, porter service if pre-booked
  • Escorting through immigration and security formalities using Fast Track Lanes wherever available
  • Escorting to the Departure Lounge if arranged and/or to the boarding gate

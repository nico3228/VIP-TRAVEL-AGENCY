Meet & Assist Service

The essence of the airport hospitality and assistance service is primarily to ensure that customers enjoy the facilities and all the conveniences that the terminal can offer, while assisting them with all airport formalities.

VIP Escape guarantees all this and more. Our Meet & Assist airport service has been adapted to the challenges encountered in today's international travels. We are here to offer you all the comfort, convenience, and luxury possible for your journeys.

If your priority is to travel in maximum comfort while still taking advantage of affordable prices, our Meet & Assist airport services are specifically designed for you. Not only will you benefit from a dedicated assistant who will smoothly guide you through all airport procedures related to arrival, transit, or departure, but thanks to the Meet & Assist services, you can also enjoy a host of other benefits.

If you have arrived at the airport, our personal assistant will greet you at the exit of your aircraft's gate and assist you through an alternative and privileged route during passenger and passport checks.

If you are departing from the airport, our agent will be at your disposal until you reach the gate - offering you all the help you need before departure.

Forget about long lines and crowds. Forget about the crowded airport environment and all the problems it causes passengers. We have thought of everything. With VIP Escape's airport services, you won't have to worry about anything. Not even about bags and luggage. The task of our baggage handlers is to offer you their assistance.

Remember that our hospitality and assistance service is very flexible and can be customized according to your specific needs, including private car transfers and chauffeur services.

MEET AND ASSIST SERVICE INCLUDE:

Arrival

  • Our agent meets the passengers at the end of the airbridge or at the entrance to the terminal with a personalized name board
  • Escorting through passport control and customs using Fast Track Lanes wherever available
  • Escorting to baggage claim hall, help with the luggage if porter service is pre-booked
  • Escorting to onward mode of transportation or arrival hall, up to the clients needs

Departure

  • Our agent and passenger's driver/lead passenger get in contact to coordinate the drop-off time and place prior to their arrival
  • Meeting passengers curbside by the agent and baggage porter if pre-booked
  • Escorting through all check-in and security formalities using Fast Track Lanes wherever available
  • Escorting to the Departure Lounge if arranged and/or to the boarding gate

Transit

  • Our agent meets the passengers at the end of the airbridge or at the entrance to the terminal with a personalized name board
  • Escorting to baggage claim if needed, porter service if pre-booked
  • Escorting through immigration and security formalities using Fast Track Lanes wherever available
  • Escorting to the Departure Lounge if arranged and/or to the boarding gate
